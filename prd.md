Date: 27 Feb 2026
Owner: Snapy/Prince
Prepared by: Senior Web Architect (no bullshit)

1. Executive Summary

This website is for a renewable energy solutions company targeting Indian residential, commercial, and industrial customers. Core focus:

Lead generation

Authority building

Solar cost and savings calculator (INR)

Highlight services: solar, biogas, AMCs (Maintenance), solar water heaters

Target audience: Indian homeowners, businesses, installers, and partners.

Primary objective: Convert visitors into qualified leads and provide instant cost/value insights via the Solar Calculator.

2. Product Vision & Goals

Vision:
Build India’s most trusted renewable energy website that educates, converts, and guides users through solar adoption.

Goals:

Educate about renewable services.

Showcase portfolio & team credibility.

Convert high-intent visitors into lead form submissions or calculator interactions.

Provide a best-in-class solar cost/savings calculator tailored for Indian users.

KPIs:

Conversion rate ≥ 10% (form submissions)

Time on calculator page ≥ 2 minutes

Bounce rate < 40%

5. Solar Calculator

This is a core differentiator — not optional.

Calculator must:

Accept monthly electricity bill (INR) or monthly kWh units

Ask for rooftop area (sq ft)

Ask for location (state/city)

Output:

Recommended Solar System size (kW)

Estimated installation cost (INR) (with/without subsidy)

Annual savings in INR

CO₂ emissions reduced

Payback period

Reference calculators:

Freyr Energy Solar Calculator example

National Rooftop Solar Calculator by Govt of India

Orb Energy solar savings calculator

Business rules:

Use Indian tariff & month kWh logic

Show subsidy estimates if available (state + central)

Must generate shareable PDF results

Must work responsively
